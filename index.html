How to play
Master the grid in 4 simple steps
1
Slide the tiles using arrow keys or by swiping. All tiles shift as far as possible in that direction.
2
Merge equal tiles — when two identical tiles collide, they combine into one with their values added together.
3
Reach 2048 by chaining merges. A new tile (2 or 4) spawns after every move. Plan ahead!
4
Game over when the grid is full and no adjacent tiles can merge. Aim for the highest score!
